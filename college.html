Our Philosophy

The journey starts with understanding who you are.

Ikigai is a Japanese concept that means "a reason for being". It is the intersection of four elements: what you love, what you are good at, what the world needs, and what you can be paid for.

At Mindful Education Consulting, we believe that every student has an ikigai, a unique purpose that drives them to make a positive difference in the world. Our goal is to help students discover their ikigai and align it with their higher education choices. We are here to support students and their families in their journey to find their ikigai and achieve their dreams.

At Mindful Education Consulting, we recognize that some students have already discovered their ikigai – their unique purpose that fuels their passion and ambition, while others are on their journey to find a path forward for college and their careers. Our services are specifically designed for these students, providing them with college and career counseling that is not only about finding the right fit but also about charting a strategic path forward.

We offer personalized college planning, assisting students in selecting courses, extracurricular activities, leadership and competitive pursuits that align with their ikigai and enhance their college applications. Understanding that the journey towards higher education and career success is a collaborative effort, we also engage with parents to comprehend their aspirations for their child.
